About Us


Dragon Karate Academy was established in 1990. We teach traditional Shotokan Karate and compete in the World Karate Federation (WKF) events, such as British & European Karate Federation Championships.


We pride ourselves on providing a fun, safe and encouraging environment for our members. Our classes develop physical fitness, mental strength, coordination and valuable social skills.


We welcome new members of all ages, join us and have fun, get fit and make new friends.

Lead Instructor


Sensei Kirk Wasserman (5th Dan Black Belt)


Kirk was raised in South Africa where he began his karate journey, representing his country at National and International levels of competition.


He has a friendly and supportive teaching style, together with his natural ability and high technical skill Kirk will ensure you reach your full potential.

Physical Benefits of Karate


Self Defence, you will learn to defend yourself if the situation arises. Develop strength, flexibility and cardiopulmonary conditioning. Burn calories, our training sessions will help you lose weight and keep you fit.

Mental Benefits of Karate


Improve your self-confidence. Karate promotes self-awareness, self-discipline and self-control. Karate promotes respect, perseverance, humility and patience. Karate reduces stress and anxiety through deep breathing, meditation, and mindfulness. You will learn to keep focused while remaining calm and alert.
