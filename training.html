Costs


The first Trial Session is free!

Membership for individuals is £30 per month. Family discounts are available, please contact the club for more information. Membership is paid monthly in advance by direct debit and can be cancelled at any time.


Grading Fees/Licence Fees

There are no Grading or Licence Fees! Cost is covered by membership. A Karate Uniform, called a “gi” can be purchased through the club.
